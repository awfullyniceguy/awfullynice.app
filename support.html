App Support and Contact

How to get help or provide feedback for our applications.

We're here to help! If you encounter any bugs, have questions, or need assistance with any of our apps, please reach out using the contact method below. We aim to respond to all support requests within 24-48 hours.

Technical Support & Inquiries

For the fastest response regarding bugs, technical issues, feature requests, or general questions, please email us directly:

Email: info@awfullynice.app

When reporting a technical issue, please include:

  • The name of the app (e.g., LobePort)
  • The device and operating system version you are using (e.g., Vision Pro, visionOS 2.0)
  • A detailed description of the problem and steps to reproduce it
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages
